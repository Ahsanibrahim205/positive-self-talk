What is positive
self-talk

  • The ability to create “automatic positive thinking systems”, (APTS) which are positive in outlook, motivating / encouraging; build confidence and assertiveness in all different kinds of situations at home; work, and socially.
  • The opposite – “automatic negative thinking systems” (ANTS) which are negative in outlook, de-motivating, selfdestructing and are non-assertive.
  • The goal is for you to develop the wellness habit of having APTS right from the outset when you find yourself in any kind of situation that leads to you experiencing tension.
  • Adopting this pattern of positive self- talk will automatically block and lessen the amount of ANTS you have to deal with.
  • Examples of ANTS that are balanced and blocked by APTS are:

    • “Oh my, this is going to be too much for me. I’ll never cope!”

    V

    “I have been in difficult situations before and managed to find solutions, so I can now”

    “When I make a mistake everyone will notice and it will be so embarrassing!”

    V

    “Even if I make mistakes I will learn from this experience and that’s good ”

    “There’s no way I will be able to do that well given those deadlines!”

    V

    “This looks tough but once I have broken it down into smaller tasks I will be in a much better position to decide a plan of action”

    “He hasn’t rung because he isn’t really interested.”

    V

    “He wants to ring me as arranged but something has just cropped up that stopped him.”

    Note: the power of APTS as you read them
    APTS are realistic and believable.
    Make your thoughts really apt!
